Nice to meet you!

I am a student at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, studying in the School of Information with a focus on user experience research and design. I'm also pursuing a minor in Spanish language and culture. Through my classes and work experience, I've gained experience creating designs focused around user needs and gained the writing and communication skills necessary to work on interdisciplinary teams.

I'm passionate about UX because I'm interested in bringing the views of different user groups together in order to create an enjoyable experience for everyone. Through talking to different types of users about the struggles they've had in using technology, I've been able to see firsthand that a user's personal experience is the most important tool in designing an intuitive system.
